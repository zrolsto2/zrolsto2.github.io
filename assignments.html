Presentation
Due: December 3rd

General Guidelines:
  • Length requirement: strictly between 18 and 20 minutes.
  • Everyone in the group must speak in the presentation for at least 2 minutes (unless otherwise cleared beforehand with the professor.)
  • Each group's presentation must utilize some form of electronic media. However, the use of videos is prohibited. Using a video will result in a significant reduction in the group's grade.
  • Each member of the group will receive the same grade. However, if you do not contribute to your group, you can lose points individually.
  • Each presentation should use at least 5 scholarly sources (no wikipedia) your presentation must include a citations slide other form of media. Citations must be in Chicago or MLA format.
  • If you are unsure about anything about the presentation, you can come by the professors office hours to discuss it.
Extra Credit:
  • Extra credit may be received if a group wears costumes themed to their particular presentation topic. Costumes must be of significant quality and cannot be disparaging to any group.
  • Extra credit may be received if a group conducts their entire presentation via puppet. Puppets cannot be made out of socks or bags and must be quality.
  • Extra credit may be received if a group conducts the entirety of their presentation via a quality rap, poem or song.
  • If you cannot do extra credit well, do not attempt it. If in your attempt to pursue extra credit, you detract from the overall quality of your presentation, you will lose points.
